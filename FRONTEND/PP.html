Welcome to our Note Saver app! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • Username & Email: Required for account creation and login.
  • Notes: Any text you paste or write in the app is saved so you can access it later.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the app’s functionality.
  • To allow you to log in and access your saved notes.
  • We do not share your data with third parties.

Storage of Notes

  • Notes are stored on our servers in plain text.
  • Site administrators can access notes if needed.

Cookies and Local Storage

  • We use local storage to remember that you have acknowledged this Privacy Policy so it won’t appear every time you visit.
