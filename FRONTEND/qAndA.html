Welcome to Notes Saver! Any questions don't be afraid to message me! Click Login to start!

Here are some common questions one may face.

  • Where can I get my notes from?
    • Click Login and then login with your information and start typing away!
  • How can I add notes?
    • Click on the left box and start typing!
  • How can I delete my saved notes?
    • Unfortunatly currently all saved notes are permant. We suggest contacting owner to delete all notes or creating a new account.
  • Why does it keep saying Internal Server Error when I have text placed in textbox?
    • We have a minimum character limit of 15 characters.
  • What does the reset button do?
    • The reset button just clears all unsaved text in the textbox.

Any suggestions? Contact Owner!
