Frequently Asked Questions

What does this website do?

Similar to Google Keep, this website allows users to securely store and access important notes from anywhere.

How can I start writing notes?

Click "Sign Up", create an account, and once logged in you can immediately begin writing and saving notes.

If I exit this site will my notes and login information be saved?

As long as you remember your username and password all the ntoes you save will be there.
